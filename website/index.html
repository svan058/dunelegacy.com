About Dune Legacy

Dune Legacy is a labor of love by dedicated developers to preserve and enhance the first-ever real-time strategy game. Based on the legendary 1992 classic Dune II by Westwood Studios, this open-source remake brings modern conveniences while faithfully preserving the gameplay that revolutionized PC gaming and spawned the entire RTS genre.
