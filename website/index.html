About Dune Legacy

Dune Legacy is an effort by developers to revitalize the first-ever real-time strategy game. Based on the 1992 classic Dune II by Westwood Studios, this remake brings modern enhancements while preserving the original gameplay that laid the foundation for the Command & Conquer series.
